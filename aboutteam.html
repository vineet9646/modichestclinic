Dr. Anuradha Bansal


Dr. Anuradha Bansal, MD Pediatrics, MRCPCH (London) Pediatrician with 17+ years of experience Founder of Healthy Kids Dr. Anuradha Bansal is a distinguished pediatrician with a wealth of experience and expertise. Her commitment to providing exceptional healthcare for children has made her a prominent figure in the field of pediatrics. Dr. Bansal holds an MD in Pediatrics and is a proud member of the Royal College of Pediatrics and Child Health (MRCPCH). With nearly two decades of clinical practice, Dr. Bansal has been a beacon of hope for countless families. She is known for her compassionate and holistic approach to pediatric care, ensuring that every child under her supervision receives the best possible treatment. Her extensive knowledge spans from newborn care to adolescent health, and she excels in diagnosing and managing a wide range of pediatric conditions. Choose Dr. Anuradha Bansal for top-tier pediatric care that combines experience, expertise, and empathy, ensuring a brighter and healthier future for your child. Your child's health, her priority.
